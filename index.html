Top 5 Tourist Attractions In China

Planning a vacation to China, but don't know where to start?

#1: Great Wall of China in Beijing

The Great Wall of China is one of China's most well-known tourist attractions. Built as a military defensive fort back in the 17th century AD, this wall took over 2,000 years to build and now stretches over 20,000 km across the northern border of China. The Great Wall of China consists of walla, watch towers, fortresses, etc.
